Cornelius Openheimer (pronounced OPE-n-heimer) was a remarkable scientist, earning "Employee of the Month" yet again. His fellow scientists would always make fun of him but on April 1st, they decided to pull the ultimate prank. They decided to push Cornelius into a gene splicing machine with an ear of corn and turn it on unplugged just to scare him. However, the machine was actually plugged in due since Cornelius was just about to start experiments on an ear of corn to make a new pesticide-resistant strain. After a huge explosion, Cornelius came out with two ears of corn for arms, and a mountain of rage. With the kernels able to heat up, shoot off his cob arms, explode, and regenerate, the humanoid GMO incinerated the lab and set off on his quest to destroy every science laboratory in the Midwest.
